Site Purpose

An important part of study, especially in ancient texts of scripture is understanding the words written. This tool will utilize a dictionary API to assist in finding the meaning behind words in scripture as well as finding their etymologies, synonyms, and antonyms.

Scenarios

  • Studying in Alma 7 and desiring to better understand the word "succor"
  • In Conference and a speaker uses an unknown word
  • Studying about Christ's Atonement, and you want to know where the word comes from
