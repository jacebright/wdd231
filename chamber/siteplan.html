Miami, AZ Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

This site provides a unified place for local businesses to unify together and promote best business practices, as well as provide a place for residents and visitors to see a collective grouping of many local businesses.

Scenarios

  • A new business in town would like some professional support in growth and promotion.
  • Visitors to town would like to see upcoming events that may be of interest
